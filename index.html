Xian Zhong

A Professor with the School of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence, WHUT and a Visiting Scholar with the ROSE@EEE, NTU

Biography

Cross-modality Analytics (X), Image Processing (I), Action Recognition (A), Neuromorphic Computing (N)

Xian Zhong(钟 忺) is currently a Professor and Ph.D. Supervisor with the School of Computer Science and Artificial Intelligence at Wuhan University of Technology (WHUT). He received Bachelor (’03–’07) degree in the School of Computer Science from Wuhan University (WHU), and Master (’07–’09) and Ph.D. (’09–’13) degrees in the School of Computer Science and Technology from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST). He served as a Visiting Scholar (’21–’22) with the School of Computer Science at Peking University (PKU) under the supervision of Prof. Tiejun Huang. Additionally, he is currently a Visiting Scholar (’23–Now) with the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering at Nanyang Technological University (NTU) under the supervision of Prof. Alex Chichung Kot.
